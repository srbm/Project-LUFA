Spring League

Played at Seneca Park near the softball fields, Spring League usually runs from mid-March to June. This year we are experimenting with a two league system. A draft league for players with experience and a league for beginners that are new to the game or want to work on their skills.

More Details Here!

Summer League

Played at Champions Park on River Rd, Summer League usually runs from June through August. It is our most popular league of the year with a wide range of playing level. This makes Summer League the most fun and has the most spirit. Get your cheers ready!

More Details Here!

Fall League

Played at Champions Park on River Rd, Fall League is usually played in September and October. With the club season winding down it is a relaxed place to work a little more on your skills and spirit before the cold hits.

More Details Here!

Winter League

Winter forces us indoors here in Kentucky but we make good use of unused gyms and provide you with two sessions to keep those throws sharp. Venues change yearly so check in with us in November.

More Details Here!

Waterfront Pickup

Meeting every Monday and Thursday at 7:30, this is Louisville's most consistent pickup game. A very laid back group that will only give you a work out if you want it. No stall. No calls. Everyone is welcome if you can adhere to their "style".

More Details Here!

Summer Pickup

This Facebook group throws together games on a regular basis but also changes days/times/places often. This game is more organized but still good natured. Everyone welcome.

Area Pickup

For those traveling to Frankfort for work, FUPU has been playing every Wednesday for the last 5 years at Capitol View Park starting at 5:30. They also play Sunday afternoon at 1PM. Very rarely, weather will force a cancellation or relocation. Updates posted on their Facebook page. This group is super friendly and laid back. All are welcome.
